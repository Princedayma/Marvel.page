IRON MAN.

Journey: After being captured by terrorists and witnessing the destruction caused by his weapons, Stark builds the Iron Man suit to escape. He later refines the suit to become Iron Man, dedicating his life to protecting the world. Stark plays a pivotal role in forming the Avengers and is the primary financier and tech provider for the team. He makes the ultimate sacrifice in "Avengers: Endgame" to save the universe from Thanos
